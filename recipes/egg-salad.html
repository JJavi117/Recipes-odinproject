How to make the best Egg Salad

Description

This easy egg salad recipe is the perfect make-ahead dish for breakfast on toast, for lunch in a wrap, lettuce wrap, over salad or a slice of bread. Sometimes I just eat it with a spoon right out of the fridge!

Ingredients

  • Boiled eggs:Use large eggs, preferably Organic and humanely raised if your budget allows.
  • Mayo: You can use regular or light mayo.
  • Tomato products:You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Scallions or chive: finely chopped
  • Spices and seasonings: Sweet paprika, salt, black pepper.
  • Sugar: Un poquito.

Steps

  1. Start by boiling the eggs. You can do this on the stove, instant pot or even the air fryer!
  2. Once cooked, run them under cold water and peel the eggs.
  3. Use an egg slice or a knife and chop the cooked eggs.
  4. Combine the cooked eggs in a bowl with mayonnaise, red or green onion, salt, pepper and stir, and garnish with paprika.
  5. Eat right away or refrigerate until ready to eat.
